In Collaboration With Rajasthan Police
In proud collaboration with the esteemed Rajasthan Police, we stand united in our commitment to security, order, and peace. Established in January 1951, the Rajasthan Police has evolved into a formidable 95,000-strong force, embodying excellence in organization, equipment, operational techniques, and a steadfast outlook. Together, we share a narrative of achievements and glory spanning seventy-three years. From combating the menace of dacoits in the Chambal ravines to addressing the challenges posed by criminal gangs, spies, smugglers, and narco-terrorists along the international border, the Rajasthan Police has consistently demonstrated resilience and dedication. Aligned with the mission of the Rajasthan Police, our collaboration strives to uphold the law, maintain societal order, and ensure the safety of communities. With values rooted in courage, fairness, loyalty, and integrity, our joint efforts inspire and create a sense of security. The Rajasthan Police, driven by dynamism and professionalism, treats its people as the most valued asset, serving them with unwavering commitment, humility, and trust. Adapting to the demands of the 21st century, the Rajasthan Police is equipped with the latest Forensic Science Laboratory, advanced Telecommunication facilities, and cutting-edge Information Technology. While the tools may have evolved, the enduring 'esprit de corps' — a shared camaraderie that defines us as a family — remains unaltered. In the true spirit of the Rajasthan Police motto, 'Sevarth Katibaddhta' सेवार्थ कटिबद्धता, we collaborate to ensure a safer and more secure future for all.
